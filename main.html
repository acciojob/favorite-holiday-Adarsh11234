Manali

Manali, a high-altitude Himalayan resort town in India’s northern Himachal Pradesh state, is a jewel in the crown of the Indian tourism industry. It’s a hub for adventure sports, a haven for nature lovers, and a sanctum for spiritual seekers.


Overview Of Manali

  1. Solang Valley: A paradise for adventure enthusiasts, this valley is famous for paragliding, skiing, and cable car rides.
  2. Hadimba Temple: This ancient temple, nestled amidst cedar forests, is known for its intricate wooden architecture and serene ambiance.
  3. Rohtang Pass: Located at an altitude of 13,050 feet, this pass offers breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains and is a gateway to Lahaul-Spiti Valley.
  4. Old Manali: A quaint village known for its bohemian vibe, traditional Himachali homes, and lively cafes with soulful music.
  5. Vashisht Hot Springs: Renowned for its natural sulfur springs, this attraction is perfect for relaxing and enjoying the majestic Himalayan view
